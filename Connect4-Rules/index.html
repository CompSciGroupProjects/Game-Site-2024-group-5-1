Hangman

Rules

This two player (player one is blue and player 2 is green) game uses a 6 by 7 gird to play connect 4. Click on a heart to fill your token in the available space; once you this, there will either be a blue or green circle in the spot that you have taken. You cannot pick a spot already taken. Each player will alternate turns. The aim of the game is to get four in a row. You can do this by getting 4 in a row diagonally, up, or down. You will win this way; you can also tie if none of the players get 4 in a row and all spots are taken. The board will disable once a player wins or a tie occurs.
