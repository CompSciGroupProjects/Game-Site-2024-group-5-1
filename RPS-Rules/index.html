Rock Paper Scissors

Rules

  • #1: The Player can pick an option consisting of: Rock, Paper and Scissors. The user chooses their option by clicking the labeled button. The user can see their option below the Enter Button
  • #2: After the user picks their choice, they must click the "Enter" button, causing the computer to pick a random choice
  • #3: Immediately after this, both the user and computer choices will be listed under the choice bar
  • #4: After the above 3 steps, the winner will be announced in one of 3 options; the options are "Tie", "Computer Wins", or "Player Wins"
  • #5: To play again with the same choice, re-click the "Enter" button, to play again with a different choice, repeat steps #1-4
