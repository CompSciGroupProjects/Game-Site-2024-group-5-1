Hangman

Rules

  • 1. There is one dash on the bottom of the game board for each letter of the word
  • 2. The player will guess one letter at a time
  • 3. If the letter is not in the word the player will lose points
  • 4. If the letter is in the word it will fill the dash where the letter is
  • 5. The game will end when the player loses all their lives or fully guess the word
